Who We Are

Rooted in
community & quality.

JNR Trading was founded in 2018 by James and Rita Ndlovu, who spotted a gap in the market for a grocery distributor that genuinely cares about product origins and client relationships.

What started as a small fruit & veg stall in Cape Town has grown into a trusted name across the Western Cape and beyond — supplying everything from staple dry goods to exotic international ingredients, always with a smile, always on time.

What Drives Us

Values We Deliver

These aren't mission-statement words. They're the principles behind every delivery, every product choice, every client call.

Farm to Fork

We build direct relationships with growers so you get produce at its peak — and farmers receive fair, sustainable prices for their work.

Partnership Mindset

We don't just take orders — we help you choose the right products, manage stock levels, and actively grow your business alongside ours.

Relentless Logistics

Our fleet runs on precision — early-morning deliveries, real-time tracking, and contingency plans built into every single route.

Uncompromising Quality

Every SKU is checked multiple times before it reaches your shelf. We refuse to distribute anything we wouldn't serve our own family.

Community First

B-BBEE Level 2 certified. We actively source from local co-ops and support township economies wherever our wheels roll.

Passion for Food

We are foodies first, distributors second. That passion drives us to constantly find new and extraordinary products for your customers.
